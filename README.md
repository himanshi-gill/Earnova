#Earnova
#Description

Lets tell your kids that awards such as new items, toys and even pocket money are not for granted. They have to earn things by doing hard work, and their carelessness or mistakes can affect this progress.

The app is for children aged 7â€“12 years. This app helps in building habits in small children and letting them understand the importance of hard work and hard-earned things, along with the proud feeling they can experience through their achievements.

It is a gamified version so kids can enjoy the application with a competitive heart while learning responsibility at the same time.

#Features

* Kids log the tasks that their parents think are rewarding.
* They get 10+ points for each completed task.
* If parents decide there is a penalty for carelessness, 15 points are deducted.
* In the Quests section, kids complete quests and earn stars.
* Stars can be used to buy their favourite cards in the Shop View.
* Quests repudiate after 12 hours and 10 coins will be rewarded after completing all quests for the day.
* In the Profile View, parents can set the total coins to be achieved.
* Kids can track their progress and see how close they are to their goal.
* Confetti animations are triggered on achievements to make it fun and rewarding.

#Tech Stack

* Swift
* SwiftUI
* State Management (`@State`, `@Binding`, `@EnvironmentObject`)
* UserDefaults Storage
* Confetti Package
* Trigger Haptic Feedback
* Fully offline functionality

#Purpose of the App

The main goal of Earnova is to build discipline and habit formation in children while keeping the experience fun and engaging.

It teaches:

* Accountability
* Consistency
* Value of hard earned rewards
* Understanding that actions have consequences
